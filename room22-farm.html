About Room 22 Farm

Room 22 is BSTM's organic farming division, committed to providing fresh, pesticide-free produce to the people of Botswana. We grow everything locally in Gaborone using sustainable farming practices.

Every purchase from Room 22 supports local agriculture, reduces food miles, and earns you extra THB rewards. It's healthy for you and healthy for the nation.
