Buying on BSTM

How do I place an order?

To place an order on BSTM Marketplace:

  1. Browse products or use the search function
  2. Click on a product to view details
  3. Click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now"
  4. Review your cart and proceed to checkout
  5. Fill in delivery information
  6. Choose payment method (Paystack, THB, or Cash on Delivery)
  7. Confirm and place your order

You'll receive a confirmation email and can track your order in your dashboard.

What payment methods are accepted?

BSTM accepts the following payment methods:

  • Paystack: Credit/Debit cards and Mobile Money
  • THoBoCoin (THB): Use your THB wallet balance
  • Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive your order

All payments are secure and encrypted.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend on your location and the delivery method:

  • CabLink Express: 1-2 hours (same day)
  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days
  • Pickup: Collect from seller's location immediately

You can track your order in real-time from your dashboard.

Can I return or refund an order?

Yes! Our return policy allows:

  • Returns within 7 days of delivery
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
  • Contact seller first to initiate return

Use the "Request Return" button in your order details or contact support.

Selling on BSTM

How do I become a seller?

To start selling on BSTM:

  1. Create an account or login
  2. Go to "Become a Seller" in settings
  3. Complete seller verification (KYC)
  4. Submit business/personal documents
  5. Wait for approval (usually 24-48 hours)
  6. Start listing products!

Verification ensures buyer trust and platform security.

What are the seller fees?

BSTM charges a simple, transparent fee structure:

  • Commission: 5% on each sale
  • Listing Fee: FREE
  • Payment Processing: Included in commission
  • Featured Listings: P10 for 7 days (optional)

You receive 95% of the sale price directly to your account.

How do I get paid?

Getting paid is simple:

  1. Buyer places order and pays
  2. Payment is held securely by BSTM
  3. You ship the product
  4. Buyer confirms delivery
  5. Payment released to your BSTM wallet
  6. Withdraw to your bank account anytime

Payments are released within 24 hours of delivery confirmation.

THoBoCoin (THB) Rewards

What is THoBoCoin (THB)?

THoBoCoin (THB) is BSTM's reward token built on BSC Testnet. It's designed to:

  • Reward users for purchases and platform activity
  • Be used as payment for products
  • Enable exclusive discounts and perks
  • Build a connected BSTM ecosystem

Conversion Rate: 1 THB ≈ P10 BWP (may vary)

How do I earn THB?

Earn THB through:

  • Purchases: Earn up to 1% THB on every order
  • CabLink Rides: Earn THB for using CabLink delivery
  • Referrals: Invite friends and earn bonus THB
  • Reviews: Write product reviews
  • Quests: Complete platform challenges

THB is automatically added to your wallet after each qualifying action.

How do I use THB?

Use THB to:

  • Pay for products: Use THB instead of BWP at checkout
  • Book CabLink rides: Pay with THB tokens
  • Get discounts: THB users get exclusive deals
  • Withdraw: Convert THB to BWP and withdraw to bank

Access your THB wallet from Settings → Payment & Wallet.
